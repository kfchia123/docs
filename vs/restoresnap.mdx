---
title: "Restoring Snapshot"
---

To restore your VPS from a Snapshot, first go to the Product details page of your selected product to do restoration, click on **Snapshots** button.

Locate the **Snapshot** which you want to restore from, click on the **Rollback** button.]

An important note, all changes made after your selected restore point will be lost.\
Click on Confirm once you are sure to proceed the restore process.
