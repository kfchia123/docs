---
title: "Billing Cycle"
---

CloudBud offers few type of billing cycles, namely

1. Monthly\
2. Quarterly ( 3 Monthly )\
3. Half Annually ( 6 Monthly )\
4. Annually ( 12 Monthly )\
5. Bi-Annually ( 24 Monthly )\
\
The longer the billing cycle, the more savings you will enjoy.\
\
Next billing cycles with the 1 month from the start of current cycle.\
For example: Current billing cycle is 3rd of January to 3rd of February, next start of cycle will be 3rd of February to 3rd of March.
