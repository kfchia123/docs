---
title: "Lost Email Used / Password"
---

Your username is the email you used to sign up for your CloudBud account.\
Understand that you may have many email and forgotten which one you used, contact our Support Team for assistance.

You can request a password reset email from the [<u>Account Manager Portal</u>](https://my.cloudbud.com/) login page. followed by the **Forgot?** link or by going directly to the [<u>password reset page</u>](https://my.cloudbud.com/password/reset).

Our system will then send you a password reset link.\
Just follow the instruction on the link to get your password reset.
